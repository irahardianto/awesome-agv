🚀 Awesome AGV v3.0.0 is here: The RMAS Evolution! 🚀

For the longest time, AI coding assistants have operated linearly—one agent executing a chain of tasks. But what happens when the scope of a feature outgrows a single context window?

Enter the **Recursive Multi-Agent System (RMAS)**. 

With Awesome AGV v3.0.0, we’ve completely reimagined the `/workflow-team` orchestration pipeline. Agents can now dynamically self-organize, spawn sub-teams hierarchically, and scale horizontally across independent workflows.

Here’s what’s new:
🏛️ **4-Tier Orchestration Hierarchy:**
L1 Overseers, L2 Rally Leads, and L3 Mission Leads break massive projects into MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) vertical slices.
 
🤖 **21 Agent Personas:**
We’ve expanded the roster by adding dedicated coordinators and an independent **Arbiter** to act as a hard compliance gate. Plus, we've upgraded our UI/UX capabilities with the new **UX Craftsman**.

🛡️ **Fault Recovery & Self-Succession:**
When an agent fails, it doesn't just crash. It escalates through a 5-level fault recovery ladder. And when an agent nears its context limits, it automatically writes a succession brief and spawns a fresh version of itself to continue the work uninterrupted. 

🛑 **Mandatory Hard Gates:**
Review and Arbitration are now non-skippable hard gates. Quality isn't an afterthought; it's architecturally enforced.

If you are using Antigravity, Roo Code, or Claude Code, you can instantly upgrade your agent's capabilities with a single command:
`npx awesome-agv --force`
